LIVING AND WORKING IN LOS ALAMOS

The Laboratory

SUMMARY

Los Alamos National Laboratory is one of the largest employeers in New Mexico employing approximatley 13,000 people. This inlcudes almost 2,500 research and development staff, 500 postdocs, and 1,600 students.

Los Alamos National Laboratory is located in beautiful Northern New Mexico about a 40 minute drive northwest of Santa Fe. The area has outstanding opportunities for the outdoor enthusiast.
